Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Who We Are

Goody's Lawn Care, LLC ("we," "us," "our") provides commercial and residential lawn care services in North Idaho. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

When you contact us by phone, text message, or through our website, we may collect:

  • Name and contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Property address and service location
  • Details about the services you're requesting
  • Text message conversation history related to scheduling
  • Call recordings or transcripts when calls are handled by our automated assistant

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and service requests
  • Schedule and confirm consultations and appointments
  • Send follow-up messages related to your service inquiry
  • Provide and improve our lawn care services

SMS & Automated Messaging

When you provide your phone number through our website chat, by phone, or in person, you may receive SMS messages from Goody's Lawn Care related to scheduling, appointment confirmations, and reminders. Message frequency varies based on your interaction. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for assistance.

No information you share with us — including your mobile phone number and SMS opt-in consent — is sold, rented, shared, or transferred to third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes. SMS opt-in consent is not shared between separate businesses or affiliates. Phone numbers and SMS opt-in are program-specific to Goody's Lawn Care, LLC.

See our SMS Consent & Disclosure page for the full opt-in disclosure.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information with service providers (such as our phone and messaging platform) solely to facilitate communication with you. These service providers are contractually prohibited from using your information for any other purpose.

Data Retention

We retain your contact information and conversation history for as long as needed to provide services and respond to inquiries. Inactive conversation data is automatically cleaned up after 72 hours.

Your Choices

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. You can request that we delete your contact information by calling us at (208) 551-6393.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us by phone at (208) 551-6393 or text our business number.
